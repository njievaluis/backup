About Us

This weather application is the result of a collaborative effort between Nji Evarus Njichuki and Nyobe Christian, software engineering 1B students at the University Institute of the Gulf of Guinea, and our dedicated instructor, Mr. Nsankong Andre.

Each of us brought our passion, creativity, and commitment to the table. Under the expert guidance of Mr. Nsankong Andre, we combined our skills to build a modern, responsive, and user-friendly weather application.

We are deeply grateful to our instructor for his mentorship, encouragement, and valuable insights throughout this project. His support was a driving force that helped us stay focused and achieve our goal.

Together, as students and mentor, we joined hands to bring this project to life — a reflection of teamwork, learning, and innovation.
